About

A dynamic company, in constant transformation.

V.I.M.E. S.R.L. is headquartered in Arienzo (CE), Italy, and was founded to excel in trade. A solid equity base, an experienced team and a profound transformation journey to maximise profitability.

Mission

Connecting global markets through efficient and innovative commercial intermediation solutions, delivering value to partners and long-term sustainability.

Vision

To be a benchmark in international trade, recognised for reliability, flexibility and the ability to generate added value.

From Retail to Import/Export

Until 2025 V.I.M.E. operated in retail. In 2026 it launched a significant strategic reorganisation to seize opportunities in the global market.

Before — until 2025

Retail trade

  • • High revenue volumes
  • • Greater operational complexity
  • • High capital absorption
  • • Inventory obsolescence risk

After — from 2026

International intermediation

  • • Asset-light model
  • • Focus on high margins
  • • Lower operational risk
  • • Greater flexibility and agility

Financial solidity
